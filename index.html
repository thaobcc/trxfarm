Frequently asked questions

How to start to invest

To start investing in trx farm, you need to install the TronLink extension for your browser. You can download it from the Chrome Web Store. If you prefer to work with the website using your smartphone, you should install the TronWallet app. You can download it from Google Play or the App Store.

How to invest on computer ?

First, you need to install the TronLink extension for the Google Chrome browser. Next, using the app, create a wallet and top up your TRX balance. Go to the trx farm website by opening a new tab in your browser. Please note that if you are logged into your TronLink wallet, you will be automatically logged into our website. By clicking on the "Investment" button, you will go to the form for creating your investment. In this form, you must indicate the amount to be invested. Make sure that you have indicated the amount you have in your wallet and click the "Invest" button.

How to invest from your mobile?

After you have installed the TronWallet application, you should create your wallet and top up your TRX balance. Click the browser icon and enter URL in the search bar. Now you can make an investment by clicking the "Invest" button and filling out the form.

How do I calculate my profit?

The income that your investment will bring can be calculated using the calculator located on the home page of the site.

How can I withdraw the dividends?

You can withdraw the accumulated dividends at any time by clicking the "Withdraw" button. Please note that with each withdrawal request, there will be a transaction fee of approximately 3 TRX. Dividends will be paid through a smart contract exclusively to the wallet from which the investment was made.

Can I create multiple investments?

Yes, you can make an unlimited investment. Each investment will be profitable separately from the others.

How to receive referral rewards?

Each trx farm user is assigned a unique referral link. You will find it in the “Referrals” section. There are also promotional materials available for you. Invite your friends and partners, and get 7 % rewards from the investment amount of your 1st level referrals and 2 % and 1 % rewards from the investment amount of your 2nd, 3rd level referrals.
